## Description

I enjoy making as much food as I can homemade, with as little ingredients as possible. Using a combination of oat and all purpose flour helps to give the crackers a better texture. And pro tip,  just grind your own  oats for the flour. You can get an entire container of whole rolled oats for a few dollars. Vs. buying a packaged bag of oat flour at an upcharge convenience price. 🤗 have a beautiful day!

Rosemary Crackers ~
Oat flour 3/4c
Allpurpose flour 3/4c
Olive oil 3tbsp
Sugar 1tsp
Salt 1tsp
Rosemary fresh or dried 1tbsp
Cold water a little less than a half cup
Adding slowly as to not over saturate the dough
Bake at 375 for 20 min


Hummus ~
Chickpeas drained 1can
Olive oil 2tbsp
Tahini 2tbsp
Sea salt 1tsp
Optional, top with Paprika, Olive oil, and Fresh
parsley.
