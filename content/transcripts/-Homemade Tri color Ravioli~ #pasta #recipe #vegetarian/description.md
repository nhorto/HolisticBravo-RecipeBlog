## Description

"We have pasta at home" Join me for an avant garde Italian dinner.  It was my first time trying 3 different pasta dough colors. It tasted amazing, but I can definitely improve the aesthetics. I used Matcha Powder for the green color,  and turmeric powder for the orange. For the filling I kept it simple blanching spinach, and mixing it with olive oil, cashews, parmesan cheese, and some salt and pepper to taste. It tastes very similar to a pesto.
