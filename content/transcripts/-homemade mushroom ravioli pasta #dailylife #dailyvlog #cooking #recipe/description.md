## Description

I make pasta at least once a week because it grounds me. I can drown out the world, and just focus on kneading the dough.

This week I made my favorite type of pasta ravioli. I stuffed it with a ricotta and mushroom mixture. For the sauce, I kept it simple with just a bit of olive oil in the pan to cook down the mushrooms.

 I then added some spices and pasta water to bring it all together with some fresh grated parmesan at the end. 

For the pasta~
All purpose flour 200g
Whole eggs 2

For the sauce~
Olive oil 1tbsp
Sliced Bella mushrooms about 4
Garlic powder, onion powder,  oregano, sea salt, and black pepper
Pasta water 3/4 cup
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup

For the filling~
Ricotta 1/2 cup
Chopped Bella mushrooms about 2
Chopped parsley few sprigs

When cooking down the Chopped mushrooms, I added a whole clove of garlic 
Minced. And seasoned with onion & garlic powder, seasalt. 
Once mushrooms and garlic are cooked down, I added it to the ricotta along with a handful of chopped parsley.  Mixed all together and transferred to a sandwich bag to pipe into the ravioli.
