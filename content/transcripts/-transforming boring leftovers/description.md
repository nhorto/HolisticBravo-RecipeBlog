## Description

Happy New Year!! 🥳
This may have been a long winded way of having dinner. But taking time to transform the leftovers you already have makes them more appealing. Especially if you're not a big fan of having the same meal prepped foods everyday. 

For the Rice I layered it in a baking dish spreading it out as thin as possible. Setting the oven to 400° let the rice cook for the first 15 min.  And the last 5 min set to broil to get a crispy top. 

I had leftover baked sweet potatoes I batch cooked for the week. I cut one up into thicker slices. Then battered with flour, liquid egg whites, and panko bread crumbs. Set those in the airfryer at 400° for 12 to 15 min.  

The chimichurri recipe comes together quickly pulsing all ingredients in a food processor or blender for 10 to 12 pulses.
