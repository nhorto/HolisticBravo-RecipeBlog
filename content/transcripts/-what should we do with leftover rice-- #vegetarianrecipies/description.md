## Description

Let's perk up some leftover rice! My leftovers is actually a mixture of rice and quinoa. Any grain would work for this. 

Start with a pan on medium heat lightly sprayed or oiled. Scramble 2 eggs till completely cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside till the end.
In the same pan add in a bag of any mixed veggies.  Cook until slightly tender. At this point you can add in the rice incorporating it with the veggies. Once mixed well we can add in seasonings.

2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce or coconut amino
1 tbsp rice vinegar (mine is lightly seasoned)
1 tsp turmeric 
1 tsp umami mushroom spice 
1 tbsp ginger paste or ginger powder 

Mix well until all seasonings are combined.
Final step is to toss the scrambled eggs back in.

This is my absolute favorite thing to do with leftover rice. It takes less than 10 min,  and makes dinner a more complete protein. And who doesn't love fried rice?! Cooking it at home allows you to make it in a healthier way, by controlling the amount of oil and sodium is added in.
