## Description

Homemade pasta always hits the spot! Especially after a long day at work. It looks more time consuming than it is. But I always aim to make dishes that can come together in 30 min or less. 

You get protein from the eggs and spinach. And it's a great way to use up a package of greens that is starting to go bad. It's like eating a salad but better 😉 

for the pasta~
a little more than half of a container of leafy greens blended with a cup of water. I used ran it through a strainer before mixing in the flour. I used 2.5 cups of flour in total. plus some extra flour to roll out the dough/to prevent sticking. 

the filling~
4 eggs
½ cup Parmesan 

Bring a pot of water to a boil, place a large bowl over the pot. Crack four eggs and whisk until they begin to get slightly thick. Add in the parmesan and continue whisking. It will form a sort of paste. You want to adjust the heat on the boiling water if you notice the eggs cooking too quickly.  Or if they are showing signs of scrambling. 

for the sauce
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp Salt
1 pepper 
1 heaping tbsp of minced garlic
the rest of the container of spinach 
1 cup of pasta water
 
Once your ravioli's begin to rise in the water you can add them to the sauce. Mixing until coated. I added some parmesan and a sprinkle of sea salt, and pepper to the final plate. Enjoy!
