## Description

Easy peasy with only 3 ingredients* if you're not adding sweetener or chocolate chips...
Almond flour 100g 
Pb powder 50g 
Optional sweetener (I used 1 tsp of monk fruit)
Chocolate chips about 1 heaping Tbsp 

Mix the pd powder in a seperate bowl with water till creamy, but still THICK. Add to almond flour, give a mix and feel free to add any inclusions you'd like. ~CANDY PIECES, SPRINKLES, M&Ms etc.
