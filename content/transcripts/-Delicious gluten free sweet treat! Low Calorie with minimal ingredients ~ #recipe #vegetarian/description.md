## Description

If you like mochi, you will LOVE these!! The consistency is amazing, and I ate the entire plate in one sitting 😋 but, fret not, each ball is only around 20 calories. It's Gluten-free and gut healthy! 

Ingredients ~
1 to 2 sweet potatoes depending on the size. 
30g tapioca flour 
10g corn starch 
20g sugar (I used monk fruit which is zero calorie sweetener) 
1 tsp Salt 

Steam your sweet potato, mash is up. Add all dry ingredients and use your hands to incorporate the mixture. It comes together very quickly. Form into ping pong size balls, and place in airfryer. I left them for 10 min on one side and 10 min on the other side. The finished product is a nice crunch on the outside and a soft mochi inside. *I also sprayed them with cooking spray on either side.
