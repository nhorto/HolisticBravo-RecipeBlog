## Description

Slow mornings call for self-care & @olnband, That can look different for everyone, this is just my version. First time making these decadent steamed eggs. They turned out so delicious 😋 I wish I had some kimchi to eat with it for a little crunch. 

2 eggs
1 C water
Scrambled warm water with eggs. Remove any air bundles with a strainer or a spoon. Steam eggs for about 8 to 10 min on low. If you're stove gets very hot,  I would let it steam with the heat on for 2 min then shut it off and steam covered for an additional 7-8 min.  

I added soy sauce, seasoned rice vinegar , and sesame seeds. 
You could also add additional spice with some fresh or crushed chillies. And/or green scallions for extra texture and flavor.
