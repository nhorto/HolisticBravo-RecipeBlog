## Description

My "lazy girl" dinner. It's amazing what you can do with a little flour and some leftover pumpkin.  I've made similar dishes with sweet potato, spinach that was close to going bad. Leftover baked potatoes. Lmk if you've tried any interesting combos! 

I measured out my leftover pumpkin which came out to be 162 grams. Generally you want to add a little more flour in grams, today I used 175 grams of APF.

 If you are using cups, it would be a cup of your "binder" (in this case; pumpkin) to a little more than a cup of flour. It doesn't have to be exact measurements.  You can incorporate the flour slowly till you get a consistent dough texture. A little springy to the touch is perfect.  

Always reserve some pasta water for your sauce. My go to is a Tbsp of olive oil.  With salt, pepper, and about a tbsp of minced garlic. Added in a cup of reserved pasta water. Finally add in your gnocchi. If you would like your sauce a little in the thick side you can add a tbsp of corn starch,  or tapioca starch to thicken it up.  I topped my pasta with fresh parmesan and parsley. 

You can definitely play with the flavors adding in additional seasonings. Like chili powder or smoked Paprika for a kick. Hope you enjoy!
