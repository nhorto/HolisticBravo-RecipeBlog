## Description

My new meal prep fixation for breakfast! So easy to make. And who doesn't love a warm egg and cheese bagel sandwich! 

High Protein Bagels~
140g flour 
140g Greek yogurt 
1tsp Salt
1tsp baking powder 
350° f in the airfryer 10 min on either side.  
Optional you can add some everything bagel seasoning to the top of them! 

For the eggs I used all egg whites. In a small pan to sit perfectly on top of our bagels.  I also added some spinach,  salt,  pepper,  and chilli powder. I went with smoked gouda for my cheese. You can use anything you like 😋
