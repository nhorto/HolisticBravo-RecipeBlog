## Description

Halloween themed desserts are always amazing,  and these no bake Gluten-free cake pops hit the spot without hurting the tummys. lmk if you'd like a more in depth recipe.

It's that time of the year where I want
pumpkin in everything I consume.
These are simple 4 ingredient gut-healthy cake pops. Made without gluten. And no baking needed. They come together in a few minutes. They are also no bake, you just pop them in the fridge for storage up to 4 days. Or freeze them for longer storage. 

Ingredients ~
Almond flour 100g
Pure pumpkin puree 50g
Monk fruit zero calorie sweetener 2tsp
Mini chocolate chips just measure with your
Sea salt to balance out the flavors.

For the white chocolate l used 2tbsp
of turmeric to get an orange color. Added benefits, turmeric is great for anti-inflammatory and a natural pain relief.
Which makes the cake pops a great snack for those of us with painful cramps.
Hope you enjoy these guilt free pops as much as I did!
