## Description

Needless to say Rex was in love with his treats. 3 simple ingredients is all you need! 

1 cup rolled oats 
¼ cup pumpkin puree 
⅓ cup peanut butter 

*make sure the peanut butter is creamy,  and the only ingredients is peanuts. No salt or additives. 

Pulse in a food processor or blender until combined.  

In the oven at 300°F for 25-30 min.  

They are delicious little treats for humans as well.
I made a similar recipe for myself, I used the same ingredients but added 2 tbsp of maple syrup.
