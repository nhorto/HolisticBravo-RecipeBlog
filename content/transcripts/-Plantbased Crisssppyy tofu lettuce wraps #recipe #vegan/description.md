## Description

These tofu wraps are full of flavor and filling without weighing you down. They come together with barely any effort. And you get protein from the tofu and the pb powder in the sauce. 

Carrot Salad~
5 Carrots shredded 
¼ of a red onion
Handful of parsley 

1 Tbsp Red wine vinegar
1 Tbsp hot honey
1 lemon
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper 

Tofu ~
Drained and cut into cubes.
Airfry at 400° for 25 min. 

½ Cup pb powder 
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp Maple syrup 
½ tbsp rice wine vinegar 
1 Tbsp black sesame seeds

Toss tofu in mixture, if mixture too think you can add a tbsp of water at a time to thin out out.  

I used butter head lettuce for the wraps but you could use romaine, or iceberg etc.
