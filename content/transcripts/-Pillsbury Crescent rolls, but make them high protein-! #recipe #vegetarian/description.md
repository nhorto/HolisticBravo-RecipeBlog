## Description

These turned out so much better than expected! They had all he delicious flakey-ness I was looking for. Let's jump into the recipe. 
100g Greek yogurt 
100g all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder 
1 tsp Salt

Set aside-
2 tbsp grated butter (I used unsalted)

I mixed the all the top ingredients together in a bowl until a ball is formed. Flattened it out to a rectangle shape. Added the grated butter in the center and folded the two sides over. Using a rolling pin to seal it up. I then folded the dough in half and rolled it out gently again. And once more I folded it in half and rolled it out, to get layers incorporated. Once done I cut the dough diagonally and rolled into a crescent shape. Popped it in the airfryer at 350F for 15 min.  And enjoy 😉
