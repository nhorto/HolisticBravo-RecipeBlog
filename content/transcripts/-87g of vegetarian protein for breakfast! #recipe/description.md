## Description

87g of protein!! This breakfast is a powerhouse of protein without any meat. And it keeps you full for hours! Don't skimp out on breakfast,  make it your most satiating meal of the day. I usually can't even think about food again for a good 6 hours after devouring this.  

Chocolate chip protein bagels~
140g bread flour 
180g Greek yogurt 
1 scoop protein powder of choice 
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp Salt
2 tbsp Mini Chocolate chips

Into the airfryer at 350° for 10 min on either side

Omelet~
3 eggs
50g egg whites 
A chunk of feta. 
Topped with half an avocado and some sea salt
