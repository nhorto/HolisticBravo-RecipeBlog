## Description

Eating something that's not only yummy, but also has protein and comes to in 5 min! High Protein Pb balls never miss. 
Pb powder mixed with water till pliable.  Rolled into balls and placed in the fridge while you're melting chocolate. I used dark chocolate mini morsals. Heated in the microwave with a tsp of coconut oil. Roll the pb balls in the chocolate and place in the fridge until set. 
Optionally you can add some sea salt on top to hit all the flavor notes.
