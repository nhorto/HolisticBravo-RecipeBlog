## Recipe Card

**Title:** High-Protein Mediterranean Veggie Wraps

**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 35 minutes
**Servings:** 4-6 wraps
**Calories:** Approximately 350-400 calories per wrap

### Description
These "lettuce-free salad" wraps are perfect for weekly meal prep. Loaded with an entire block of tofu and a full can of black beans for serious protein, then packed with Mediterranean flavors from sun-dried tomatoes, kalamata olives, pepperoncini, and feta cheese. The wraps are spread with red pepper hummus and pesto, then baked until crispy and warm.

### Ingredients

**Main Filling:**
- 1 entire block of tofu (14-16 oz)
- 1 entire can of black beans, drained and rinsed
- Seasonings, to taste

**Mediterranean Mix:**
- Sun-dried tomatoes
- Kalamata olives
- Pepperoncini
- Feta cheese, crumbled

**Spreads:**
- Red pepper hummus
- Pesto

**Wraps:**
- Large tortillas or wraps

### Instructions

1. **Prepare the Protein Base:** Drain and press tofu to remove excess water. Crumble or cube the tofu. Season to taste (suggestions: garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cumin, salt, pepper).

2. **Cook the Tofu:** In a pan over medium-high heat, cook seasoned tofu until golden and crispy. Set aside.

3. **Prepare Black Beans:** Drain and rinse black beans. Season them similarly to the tofu.

4. **Prepare Mediterranean Toppings:** Chop sun-dried tomatoes if needed. Slice kalamata olives. Slice or chop pepperoncini. Crumble feta cheese.

5. **Assemble the Wraps:** Lay out tortillas. Spread red pepper hummus on one half and pesto on the other half of each tortilla.

6. **Add Fillings:** Layer the cooked tofu, black beans, sun-dried tomatoes, kalamata olives, pepperoncini, and feta cheese down the center of each wrap.

7. **Wrap and Fold:** Fold in the sides and roll up tightly.

8. **Bake:** Place wraps seam-side down on a baking sheet. Bake at 425°F for 20 minutes until crispy and golden.

9. **Serve or Meal Prep:** Enjoy immediately or let cool and store for meal prep throughout the week.

### About the Ingredients

**Entire Block of Tofu:** Provides substantial plant-based protein. When seasoned well and cooked until crispy, tofu absorbs all the Mediterranean flavors.

**Black Beans:** Add fiber and additional protein, making these wraps extremely filling and satisfying.

**Mediterranean Ingredients:** Sun-dried tomatoes add umami sweetness, kalamata olives bring brininess, pepperoncini adds tang and heat, and feta contributes creamy saltiness.

**Dual Spreads:** Red pepper hummus adds creaminess and a mild pepper flavor, while pesto brings herbal, garlicky notes.

### Tips & Variations

- Press tofu well to get it extra crispy when cooking
- Season the tofu and beans generously - they need flavor!
- Can use any tortilla size, but larger ones hold more filling
- Baking makes the wraps crispy and holds them together better than eating fresh
- Make a big batch on Sunday for the entire week
- Reheat in oven or toaster oven for best results (microwave works but won't be crispy)
- Can substitute chickpeas for black beans
- Add spinach or arugula for extra greens
- Make it vegan by omitting feta or using vegan feta

### FAQ

1. **How long do these keep in the fridge?** Up to 5 days when stored in airtight containers.

2. **Can I freeze these wraps?** Yes! Wrap individually in foil or plastic wrap, freeze for up to 2 months. Reheat in oven from frozen.

3. **What if I don't like tofu?** Substitute with tempeh, chickpeas, or white beans for similar protein content.
