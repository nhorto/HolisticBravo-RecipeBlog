## How Affiliate Links Work

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. The retailer pays the commission — your price stays the same whether you use our link or not.

These commissions help support the site and allow us to continue creating free recipes and content.

## Our Promise

We only recommend products we personally use, love, and believe in. Affiliate relationships never influence our recipes, ingredient choices, or recommendations. If we link to a product, it's because we genuinely think it's worth your time — not because of a commission.

We will never recommend a product solely because it offers a higher commission.
