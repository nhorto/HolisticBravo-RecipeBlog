## Description

Is it just Hispanic households that are haunted by the ghosts of beans past?!? For real though, this was my favorite thing that I've made so far! I have to thank the GOAT @_justcallmenat for suggesting it! This bean salad not only tastes delicious but it packs a nutrient punch! Protein, iron, magnesium, zinc! It's high in fiber so it keeps you satiated. Girls with PCOS or high cortisol this is a great dish to add to your weekly Meal Prep. 

For the dressing I kept it simple using~
Apple cider vinegar 1tbsp
Olive oil 1tbsp
Stone ground mustard 2tbsp
Hot honey 2tbsp
Sea salt 1tsp
Juice of 1 Lemon

The fun part of this salad is you can add as many or as little beans as you'd like. I just love all types of beans so used Garbanzo, Cannellini, Black, & Edamame. Same goes with the additional veggies. I added cucumber, red onion, and tri-colored peppers. But you can add tomatoes, zucchini, carrots, radishes, jalapeño if you're feeling spicy 🌶 The options are endless. The longer the dish sits with the dressing in the fridge the more it absorbs. I also add the feta at the end. That part is completely optional as always. I'm a vegetarian, but I love all the vegan friends 🧡
