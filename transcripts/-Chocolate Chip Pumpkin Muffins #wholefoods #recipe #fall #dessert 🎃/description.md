Recipe~
Dry ingredients 
All purpose flour 150g
Oat flour 70g
Baking soda 1g
Salt 1tsp
Pumpkin pie spice 1 tbsp
Mini Chocolate chips (I never measure, you'll know when it's enough)

Wet ingredients 
1 egg
1 can of pure pumpkin puree
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Maple syrup 2 tbsp 
Egg whites 50g (this is optional I just like the extra protein) you can add an additional whole eggs instead.  
Vanilla extract 1 tsp 

I just mix everything together in a bowl, I have a non stick muffin pan. If you do not have one use liners or nonstick spray. 
Into the oven at 425F for 20-25 min.  Enjoy!
