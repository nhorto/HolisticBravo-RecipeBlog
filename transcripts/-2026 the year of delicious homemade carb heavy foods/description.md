## Description

Carbs aren’t the enemy. The confusion around them is. In this video, we break down why cutting out carbs completely isn’t the secret to sustainable weight loss, and how doing so can actually make your journey harder. Instead, the real power comes from understanding what you’re eating and taking back control of your ingredients.

By learning to make simple staples like pasta, bread, and other carb‑rich foods at home, you can enjoy the meals you love without the mystery additives, excess sugars, or unnecessary oils often found in store‑bought versions. When you know exactly what’s going into your food, you can enjoy carbs with confidence, stay satisfied, and build habits that support long‑term, realistic weight loss.

This isn’t about restriction,  it’s about empowerment, balance, and creating a lifestyle you can actually maintain.
