## Description

This is my new obsession! You could eat an entire spaghetti squash for only 300 calories! It doesn't get better than that! 

Cut your squash in half, scoop out the seeds from the center. And roast it face down on a lined pan in the oven at 425° for 45-50 min. I prefer mine a little al dente so I took it out around the 40 min mark. 

Béchamel sauce is cooked in a pan on low heat. 2tbsp of butter, stirring until melted.  2tbsp of flour, stirred into the butter until dissolved.  And a cup of milk of choice.  I used unsweetened almond milk. Slightly warm the milk in the microwave for 30-45 seconds before incorporating into your butter/flour mixture. Once the sauce has thickened add some salt, & pepper.

This sauce comes together very quickly. Have all ingredients near by. The entire process only takes 4-5 min.  Top with some parmesan and parsley and enjoy 😉
