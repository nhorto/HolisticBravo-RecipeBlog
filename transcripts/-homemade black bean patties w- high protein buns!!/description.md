## Description

Never going back to store bought veggie patties again! These were so easy to make and they turned out delicious! After counting the ingredients I realized there are in fact  ingredients in the patties, not 4 like I had stated... 😅 

Ingredients incoming..

High Protein buns~
350g bread flour
350g Greek yogurt 
1g baking powder 
Pinch of salt
Optional to add sesame seeds on top

Mix all ingredients until combined. I cut the dough into 4 pieces and molded them into bun-like shapes. I started them on 350°f for 20 min.  I then bumped the heat to 425°f for another 10 min till the top started slightly browning. 

Black beans buns~
Quarter of a red onion 
1 can black beans
⅓ cup egg whites
1⅓ cup bread crumbs (I used panko)
1 tbsp low sodium soy sauce 

After rinsing and draining the black beans, add them to a food processor with the red onion. Pulse until the onion and beans break up into chunks. You may have to stop in-between to scrap the sides down. 
Once onion is slightly chopped add the remainder of the ingredients until the paste is thick enough that it passes the upside test.  

Use parchment paper so the patties don't stick to the cutting board, this mixture will make 4 decent size patties.  Form with your hands, then place in a pan to cook. I used a bit of cooking spray so they wouldn't stick. About 3 to 4 min on either side. On low to medium heat covered. 

Add any and every topping you would like! I used shaved parmesan, pesto, arugala, & onion.
