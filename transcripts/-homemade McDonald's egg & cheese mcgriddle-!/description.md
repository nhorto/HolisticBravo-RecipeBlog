## Description

This was so good I nearly forgot to take a picture.  I had to rotate the plate because the other side already had a large bite on it.  😅 needless to say I highly recommend giving this a try.  It takes less time than you think. From start to finish it was less than 15 min.  In the future I would like to experiment with some chocolate chip pancakes as the "buns". I do love me some savory and sweet breakfasts. 

Pancake bun ingredients ~
1 cup All purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder 
1 whole egg
1 tbsp monk fruit sweetener 
1 tbsp olive oil 
Pinch of salt
For the wet ingredient you can use water or milk of choice.  I added a Tbsp at a time until I got to the consistency that I liked. You want the batter to be in the thicker side.  

Once the batter and eggs are in the molds I covered the pan and let it cook on medium to low heat for 3 to 4 min on either side. You can add a splash of water in the pan to create a steam effect. This will help the eggs to set quicker, and make it easier to flip them without creating a mess. 

The molds used in the video are linked in my Amazon storefront.
