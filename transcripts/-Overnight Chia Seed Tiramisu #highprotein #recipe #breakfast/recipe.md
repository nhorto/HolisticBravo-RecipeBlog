### Description

When you have to wake up at 1:00 a.m. for a 12-hour shift, you need a breakfast that's already prepared, packed with protein, and includes a shot of espresso for that extra energy. This overnight chia seed tiramisu delivers all that with tiramisu-inspired flavors. Layers of chia-Greek yogurt mixture with melted white chocolate and a shot of espresso create a breakfast that gives you life even on the hardest mornings. Make it the night before and grab it on your way out the door.

### Ingredients

**For the Chia Base:**
- 350g Greek yogurt
- 40g chia seeds
- Monk fruit sweetener, to taste (or honey/maple syrup)
- 1 shot espresso (or strong coffee)

**For the White Chocolate Layer:**
- 30g white chocolate, melted
- Small amount of Greek yogurt (for mixing)

**Optional Toppings:**
- Cocoa powder
- Coffee beans
- Additional white chocolate shavings

### Instructions

1. **Prepare Chia Base:** In a bowl or container, combine 350g Greek yogurt and 40g chia seeds. Mix well.

2. **Add Sweetener:** Add monk fruit sweetener to taste. You can adjust sweetness level based on preference - more or less than shown.

3. **Add Espresso:** Brew a shot of espresso (or make very strong coffee). Let cool slightly, then mix into the chia-yogurt mixture.

4. **Prepare White Chocolate Layer:** Melt 30g white chocolate (microwave in 15-second intervals, stirring between). Mix with a small amount of Greek yogurt to create a smooth, pourable consistency.

5. **Layer:** In your serving container, create layers of the chia mixture and the white chocolate yogurt. You can do stripes, swirls, or whatever pattern you like.

6. **Refrigerate Overnight:** Cover and place in refrigerator. Let it set overnight (or at least 4-6 hours) so chia seeds can absorb liquid and swell.

7. **Morning Grab-and-Go:** In the morning, give it a quick stir if desired, or eat it layered. It should smell amazing and look even better than you do at 1:00 a.m.!

### About the Ingredients

**Greek Yogurt:** The protein powerhouse that makes this breakfast sustaining through long shifts. 350g provides significant protein.

**Chia Seeds:** Absorb liquid overnight to create a pudding-like texture. Add fiber, omega-3s, and additional protein.

**Monk Fruit Sweetener:** Zero-calorie natural sweetener that doesn't spike blood sugar - perfect for sustained energy. Can substitute honey or maple syrup if preferred.

**Espresso Shot:** Not just for flavor - this gives you the caffeine boost you need when waking up in the middle of the night for work.

**White Chocolate:** Adds the "tiramisu" element with its sweet, vanilla-like flavor that contrasts with the coffee.

### Tips & Variations

- Make multiple servings at once for the whole work week
- Adjust sweetness level - use more or less sweetener based on preference
- Can use regular sugar, honey, or maple syrup instead of monk fruit
- Dark chocolate instead of white chocolate for less sweetness
- Add a layer of cocoa powder between layers for authentic tiramisu look
- Top with crushed coffee beans for texture and extra coffee flavor
- Can use regular yogurt but protein content will be much lower
- Make it in mason jars for perfect grab-and-go portions

### FAQ

1. **Can I use regular yogurt instead of Greek?** You can, but Greek yogurt is essential for the high protein content (52g). Regular yogurt has much less protein.

2. **What if I don't have monk fruit sweetener?** Use honey, maple syrup, agave, or regular sugar. Adjust amount to your taste preferences.

3. **How long does this last in the fridge?** Make on Sunday for the work week - it will last 3-4 days refrigerated in airtight containers.
