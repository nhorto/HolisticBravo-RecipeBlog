High Protein Bagels that can be transformed into several different flavor combos. The base is a simple 3 ingredients.  From their you can get creative with the inclusions.  I made two savory options and one sweet option. It was my first time turning this recipe into a dessert style bagel. It turned out so delicious I forgot to press record on my original ending. Eating them warm is my favorite way to enjoy them. You can store the uncooked bagels in the fridge for a week and pop in the air fryer when you're feeling like eating a snack that also happens to be high Protein. 

Base ingredients -
All purpose flour 100g
Non fat Greek yogurt 100g
Baking powder 1tsp
Salt 1tsp 

Matcha Cranberry bagels-
matcha powder 5g
Cranberries 30g

Double chocolate bagels-
@live.momentous chocolate protein 1scoop
Mini chocolate chips 3/4c
*I added a little more Greek yogurt to this one about a spoon full at a time till the mixture forms a solid ball. 

Once you have your dough mixed and seperated into two pieces you can form them into bagel shapes. I added everything bagel seasoning to my bagels that had no inclusions. 
Into the airfryer at 350°f / 8 min on either side. I enjoyed mine with salted butter and honey.
