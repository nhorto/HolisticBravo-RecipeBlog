## Description

I've never actually tried a chicken pot pie before. I've been a vegetarian for over two decades now. And that's just one of those dishes like meatloaf that I never got around to trying, because I was a picky eater as a kid.  Also growing up in a Hispanic household those weren't really foods that were ever in our dinner rotation.  I thought it would be fun to try a variation of a pot pie, that is plant based and full of yummy herbs and veggies. It turned out better than I could have imagined.  All the fall flavors are there.  Plus the flakey biscuit on top! You can't go wrong!
