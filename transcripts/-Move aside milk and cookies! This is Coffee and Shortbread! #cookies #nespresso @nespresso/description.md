## Description

Most of you agreed it was too early for Christmas.  But life is about doing what makes you happy. So I'm celebrating the start of the season with some Gingerbread @nespresso @nespressousa and Shortbread cookies!


My cookies unfortunately did not keep their shape, and it is completely my fault for being inpatient.  I didn't take the butter out of the fridge in advance, so instead I microwaved it to soften 🤦‍♀️🤦‍♀️ also you're supposed to let the dough set in the fridge for at least 30 min.  But again I couldn't wait, I think I left it in their for 10 min at the most.  

Ohh well, I'll try again another day. The flavors were still there though!  The cookies turned out buttery and soft on the inside. With crunchy edges. 😋 

Best part is only 3 ingredients are required! 
1.5 sticks of butter 
1/2 cup of powdered sugar
1.5 cups of all purpose flour

Doesn’t get easier than that! In the oven at 350F for 12-15 min.
