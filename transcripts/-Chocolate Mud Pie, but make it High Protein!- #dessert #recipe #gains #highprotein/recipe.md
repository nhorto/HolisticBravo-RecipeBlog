### Description
This quick high-protein sweet treat can be made in 5 minutes or less. It's delicious, satisfying, and perfect for anyone looking to satisfy their sweet cravings while hitting their protein goals. The consistency is like a chocolate mousse or mug pie.

### Ingredients
- 170g non-fat Greek yogurt
- 1 scoop chocolate protein powder (Momentous brand used in video)
- 2 tbsp mini chocolate chips

### Instructions
1. In a bowl or mug, combine 170g non-fat Greek yogurt with 1 scoop of chocolate protein powder.
2. Mix thoroughly until well combined and smooth.
3. Fold in 2 tbsp mini chocolate chips.
4. Place in freezer for desired consistency (can eat immediately or freeze for a firmer texture).
5. Enjoy your high-protein chocolate treat!

### About the Ingredients
Non-fat Greek yogurt is incredibly high in protein and creates a creamy, mousse-like base. Chocolate protein powder adds flavor and boosts the protein content while keeping calories reasonable. Mini chocolate chips provide texture and satisfy chocolate cravings with just a small amount.

### Tips & Variations
- Freeze for 30-60 minutes for a firmer, ice cream-like texture
- Try different protein powder flavors (vanilla, peanut butter, cookies and cream)
- Top with fresh berries for added nutrition
- Add a tablespoon of peanut butter for PB cup flavor
- Use sugar-free chocolate chips to reduce sugar
- Mix in crushed graham crackers for a s'mores vibe
- Double the recipe for a larger serving

### FAQ
1. **Can I use regular yogurt instead of Greek?** Greek yogurt is much higher in protein and has a thicker consistency. Regular yogurt will be thinner and have less protein.
2. **How long does this keep in the fridge?** Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days. The texture is best when fresh.
3. **Is this actually filling?** Yes! The high protein content from Greek yogurt and protein powder is very satisfying and helps curb sweet cravings.
