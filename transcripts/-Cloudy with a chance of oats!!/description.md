## Description

I was definitely getting over a cold while filming this video. However even when I'm not feeling my best I still try to muster up the energy to cook for myself. And oatmeal is one of those cozy foods that fills me up and magically makes me feel better.   

Hope everyone had a fun and safe new year!! 

For the oats I used 1 cup Water to 1 cup oats. Cooked on medium to low heat till fully absorbed.  I topped my oatmeal with frozen wild blueberries,  sliced almonds, agave, and a generous about of creamy peanut butter.
