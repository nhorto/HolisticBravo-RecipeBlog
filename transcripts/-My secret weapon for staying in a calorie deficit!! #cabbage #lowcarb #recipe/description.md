## Description

This is one of the veggies that helped me stay in a calorie deficit when I was loosing weight! You can do so much with it. And it keeps you satisfied for hours. 

Pickled cabbage ~
1 Tbsp salt
1 Tbsp sugar
1 cup apple cider vinegar 
2 cups hot water
Let sit in the fridge overnight before you use it. Lasts in the fridge for about 2 weeks. I love eating a spoonful of this when I wake up in the morning.  It gives me a jolt, and makes me feel like I'm kick starting my metabolism.  

Cabbage noodles ~
Boil a pot of water with lots of salt
1 Tbsp curry powder 
1 Tbsp garlic powder 
1 Tbsp onion powder 
1 tsp ginger powder 
1 tsp chili powder 
1 tsp red pepper flakes 
1 tsp cilantro flakes 
1 Tbsp red wine vinegar (any vinegar would work)
1 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce 

I let the cabbage soak in the spices on the stove for about 30 min until fork tender. But still slightly al dente. Additionally you can add some parmesan cheese on top or omit it if you're vegan. I'm sure nutrional yeast would also be great on top of these ca-noodles. 

Eggs in a basket ~ 
Add a handful of cabbage to a pan on medium to low heat, cover and let cook a few min before adding your egg. I made a little hole in the middle for my egg to lay in, added egg whites directly on top of the cabbage to hold it together (and for extra protein!)  Season to taste. I kept it simple with sea salt and pepper.  You can add this on top of toast, or serve with rice or any grain you'd like.
