## Description

I decided to make Shortbread ginger man cookies again. I think they turned out much better this time.  I took my time, and let the dough sit in the fridge for a whole hour. I'm a bit of a sugar addict.  I pretty much always need something sweet at the end of the day. It doesn't have to be anything big. Just a little something to signal to my body we're done eating for the day. 😉

1½c flour
1½ sticks of butter room temp
½c powdered sugar 
Combine and place the dough in the fridge covered for at least an hour. 
325° 12-15min
