## Description

holisticbravo Pasta for dinner?? Yes please! I think I enjoy the taste of pasta made with water over the pasta with eggs. It absorbs more of the sauce, and the texture was better. Still a bit al dente but softer. I'm a fan of keeping it simple when it comes to the
sauce for a pasta dish. 4 ingredients or less is
usually all you need. I had a pack of
tomatoes that needed to be used. They truly were the star of the show. The portion that was cooked in the airfryer had a sweeter jammy taste to them. While the stovetop
tomatoes gave all the vummy acidity. For the
tomatoes on the stove I added olive oil to base some salt and pepper to taste and a portion of pasta water to bring it together. You could definitely leave it there if you'd like. But if you happen to have a block of parmesan lying around that truly helps the sauce come together in the best way possible.
