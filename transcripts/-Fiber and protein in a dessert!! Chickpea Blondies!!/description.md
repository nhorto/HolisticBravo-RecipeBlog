# |Fiber and protein in a dessert!! Chickpea Blondies!!

**Video ID:** 4PAaMJueKJE
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4PAaMJueKJE

## Description

These are my new favorite sweet treat! Anytime I can throw a bunch of ingredients into a blender or food processor and have it turn into something this good, it's a win! 

And the chickpea water turned into the best whipped topping. Fluffy and luxurious, I also add the topping to my pancakes or even into hot chocolate for a creamy taste. 

Blondie Base~
I can of chickpeas (drained) 
½ Cup maple syrup 
⅓ cup nut butter of choice (I used pb powder for a lower calorie option)
1 tbsp vanilla extract 
1 tsp baking powder 
1 tsp baking soda
½ tsp Salt 
*optionally you can top with chocolate chips

Aquafaba/ chickpea water~

Liquid from the can of chickpea
1 tsp of Lemon juice or vinegar ( this helps to form stronger peaks when whipping)
4 tsp powdered sugar (you can add more or less depending on how sweet you want it)

Whip the water with lemon juice or ginger alone until the mixture begins to turn fluffy.  They incorporate a tsp of sugar at a time.  Whipping gently to get stiff peaks. Usually takes about 5 min.  For best results store in the freezer.
