## Description

Chinese souffle eggs!! If you're bored of traditional scrambled eggs why not try something new?? These are actually very easy to make. The hardest part is having patience to let the eggs cooking at a slow rate. The texture is similar to a flan, very smooth and delicious on the palette. 

All you need is 2 eggs, ⅓ cup of boiling water and LOVE ❤️ 

If you don't have a steam rack their are plenty of alternatives I've seen. You can put less water in and a higher bowl. As long as you can cover the pan with a lid.
