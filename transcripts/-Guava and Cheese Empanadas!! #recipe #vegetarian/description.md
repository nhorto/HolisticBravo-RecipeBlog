## Description

Guava and cheese empanadas!! These have to be my favorite way to make empanadas. Great for anyone who loves that salty and sweet taste wrapped in crunchy deliciousness. 

Ingredients ~~

2½ c flour
1 stick butter cold
1 egg room temp
1 tsp Salt 
½ cup cold water

Add salt and flour to a large bowl. Butter should be cold and cut into cubes, use your hands to incorporate the butter with the flour. Once the mixture is crumbly you can add I scrambled egg, slowly add the water in till the dough comes together.  Use your hands to roll it into a ball. Let it sit in the fridge covered for 30min to an hour.  I cut it into 10 equal pieces. Rolled into balls. Use a rolling pin to flatten. 

For the inside I used guava paste cut into slices. And parmesan cheese also cut into thick slices.
